What HISPA does

We help students see it, so they can become it.

Everything we do flows from a single insight: young people aim higher when they meet someone who shares their background and has already walked the path ahead.

01 · Flagship

The Role Model Program

Latino professionals visit middle-school classrooms over a series of sessions, sharing their educational and career journeys — the obstacles, the doubts, and the decisions that got them there. Students don't just hear about success; they meet it face to face.

  • ✓ Structured, repeat classroom visits
  • ✓ Bilingual volunteers for English learners
  • ✓ Goal-setting and reflection activities

02 · Annual

Youth Leadership Conferences

We bring students onto college campuses for a day that makes higher education feel real and within reach — workshops, panels of Latino leaders, and a campus that suddenly feels like it could be theirs.

  • ✓ Hosted on university campuses
  • ✓ Hands-on leadership workshops
  • ✓ Families invited into the journey
03 · Ongoing

The Leadership Network

Role models and alumni stay connected through a growing community — sharing opportunities, mentoring one another, and ensuring that the relationship a student forms today keeps paying forward for years.

  • ✓ Volunteer training & support
  • ✓ Alumni mentorship circles
  • ✓ Corporate & community partners

How a partnership works

From first visit to lasting change
