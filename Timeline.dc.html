Our timeline · est. 2007

A journey written in the stars.

Nearly two decades of showing up for students — one milestone at a time. Keep scrolling to travel through the HISPA story.

Begin ↓
2007
The launch

HISPA is founded

Dr. Ivonne Díaz-Claisse launches HISPA and recruits the first Latino professionals to step into classrooms and share their journeys.

2009
First orbit

First Youth Conference

Students step onto a college campus for the first HISPA youth leadership conference — and higher education suddenly feels real.

2011
Expansion

Expanding the reach

The Role Model Program grows across New Jersey and into New York City schools, reaching thousands more students.

2013
Constellation

The Three Kings Banquet

An annual celebration begins — honoring Latino leaders and rallying the community behind the mission.

2015
Alignment

Joining forces for Camden

Corporate and university partners rally behind bilingual classroom visits for English learners.

2020
Deep space

Showing up, virtually

When schools close, HISPA pivots to virtual visits — proving the connection travels through any screen.

2023
Full circle

The students come back

Former HISPA students — now graduates and professionals — return as role models themselves.

2026
The north star

40,000+ students later

The mission is the same as launch day — only now it reaches further than Ivonne ever imagined. And the journey is still climbing.
